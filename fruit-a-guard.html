The Challenge

Fruit growers face numerous challenges that impact yield and quality

Pest Damage

Insects and birds cause significant fruit damage, leading to 30-40% crop losses in many orchards

Weather Damage

Hail, strong winds, and excessive rain can bruise or damage fruits, reducing market value

Disease Spread

Fungal and bacterial infections spread rapidly, especially in humid conditions

Chemical Dependence

Excessive pesticide use raises health concerns and increases production costs

Our Solution

Biodegradable Fruit Protection System

Fruit-A-Guard is a patented, eco-friendly protective covering system that provides comprehensive protection for fruits from the early growth stage until harvest. Made from 100% biodegradable materials, it creates a protective microenvironment that shields fruits from pests, diseases, and adverse weather conditions.

Developed through extensive research and field trials, Fruit-A-Guard significantly reduces the need for chemical pesticides while improving fruit quality, color development, and marketable yield.

Key Features

What Makes It Special

Innovative features for comprehensive fruit protection

100% Biodegradable

Made from natural plant-based polymers that completely decompose within 6-12 months, leaving no harmful residues in the soil.

Breathable Design

Micro-perforations allow air circulation while keeping pests out, preventing moisture accumulation and fungal growth.

UV Protection

Filters harmful UV rays while allowing optimal sunlight transmission for proper fruit coloration and ripening.

Water Resistant

Protective coating repels excess water while maintaining breathability, preventing water damage and disease spread.

Easy Installation

Simple slip-on design that can be easily installed on individual fruits without requiring special tools or training.

Temperature Regulation

Helps maintain optimal fruit temperature, protecting against heat stress and cold damage during critical growth stages.

How It Works

Simple installation process for maximum protection

1

Identify & Select

Identify fruits at the appropriate size (typically when fruit reaches 2-3 cm diameter)

2

Install Guard

Gently slip the Fruit-A-Guard over the fruit, ensuring proper coverage and secure fit

3

Monitor Growth

The guard expands with fruit growth, providing continuous protection throughout development

4

Harvest & Remove

At harvest, simply remove the guard - it can be composted or left to biodegrade naturally

Technical Specifications

Dimensions & Variants

  • Small: 5-8 cm diameter (berries, small fruits)
  • Medium: 8-12 cm diameter (apples, peaches)
  • Large: 12-15 cm diameter (mangoes, citrus)
  • Extra Large: 15-20 cm diameter (pomelo, guava)

Material Properties

  • Material: Plant-based biopolymer blend
  • Thickness: 0.5-0.8 mm
  • Biodegradation Time: 6-12 months
  • Temperature Tolerance: -5°C to 55°C

Performance Features

  • UV Protection: 80% UV blocking
  • Water Resistance: Hydrophobic coating
  • Air Permeability: High (micro-perforated)
  • Pest Protection: 70-85% reduction
