Our Solution

Natural, Cost-Effective Growth Medium

Corn Soya Based Media is a revolutionary growth medium developed from unrefined, locally-sourced corn and soya ingredients. This innovative formulation provides all essential nutrients required for optimal fungal growth and subculturing while being economically viable and environmentally sustainable.

Through extensive research and optimization, we've created a media that not only matches but often exceeds the performance of expensive commercial alternatives, making fungal cultivation more accessible and sustainable.

Technical Specifications

Composition

  • Base Material: Unrefined corn and soya flour
  • Protein Content: 18-22%
  • Carbohydrate Content: 55-60%
  • Moisture: 8-10%

Physical Properties

  • Form: Fine powder
  • pH Range: 6.0 - 6.5
  • Color: Light brown to beige
  • Shelf Life: 12 months (dry storage)

Application Parameters

  • Concentration: 20-30 g/L
  • Sterilization: 121°C, 15-20 minutes
  • Incubation Temp: 25-30°C
  • Culture Types: Solid state fermentation
