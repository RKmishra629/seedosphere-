Our Solution

Transforming Waste into Wellness

Orange Jelly is an innovative product that transforms agricultural waste into a valuable, nutrient-rich food product. Made entirely from waste orange peel, this jelly harnesses the natural antioxidant and antimicrobial properties of orange peel compounds, including flavonoids, pectin, and essential oils.

Our proprietary extraction and processing method preserves the bioactive compounds while creating a palatable, shelf-stable product with multiple applications in food, nutraceutical, and cosmetic industries.

Technical Specifications

Composition

  • Main Ingredient: Waste orange peel extract
  • Pectin Content: 15-20%
  • Flavonoid Content: 8-12 mg/g
  • Essential Oils: 2-4%

Physical Properties

  • Form: Semi-solid gel/jelly
  • Color: Orange to amber
  • pH: 3.5 - 4.5
  • Shelf Life: 12 months (refrigerated)

Nutritional Info (per 100g)

  • Calories: 45-60 kcal
  • Vitamin C: 25-35 mg
  • Dietary Fiber: 3-5 g
  • Antioxidant Capacity: High ORAC value
